Sports

In the following section I will discuss all of my sporting interests and events I have partaken in.

Running

As a younger child, I never really took part in any activities due to my troubles with dyspraxia.

However, in my late adolescence I began to grow a fondness for running in local races.

I began to participate in running when I was 16, and have since entered into annual 5k run.

Partaking in runs has encouraged me to live a more active and health-conscious lifestyle.


Gym

Along with running, I would also stick to an exercise regieme at home to help me press forward and improve my general fitness.

However, once I began attending college this was no longer possible since I was away from home and my current residence is too small a space to properly have a workout.

Thus I started checking out the local gym, the Maridyke complex, which the college provides free membership to for its students.

It is difficult to find a balance between the studies of college and society activities to get enough time in the gym. But when the time arises I try to make the best of it.


Ultimate Frisbee

As part of my first semester, I decided to try out the frisbee club.

In the beginning I found it quite fun and entertaining, thinking it would be a nice alternative to the gym.

This continued until the beginning of the second semester, where I felt my time management was going poorly.

Thus I had to drop it for this term. I hope to return to it sometime in the future as I did enjoy my time with the team
