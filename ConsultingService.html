Introduction

Welcome to the Speedwagon Foundation consulting service.

This is a consultation service focused on informing customers on the latest technologies

available in the market.

Named after the great Robert E.O. Speedwagon, an entrepreneur from the 20th Century,

we plan to provide information to our consumers surrounding the areas of Blockchain,

Autonomous Things and Immersive Experiences.


Blockchain

Blockchain is a time-stamped series of immutable record of data that is mangled by cluster

of computers not owned by a single entity.

Blockchain is mostly synonymous with the creation of crypto-currencies such as BitCoin,

which have been highly profitable in recent times.


Autonomous Things

Autonomous Things are the term for technological developments that will bring computers to the

working environment without humans operating them.


Immersive Experiences

Immersive Experiences are the implementation of technologies to illicit a realistic feeling of

being present in a virtual space.

This technology has been in development for decades, but have grown quite popular in recent times

thanks to the availability of more advanced VR technology in the public market such as the Oculus Rift

and the HTC Vive, amongst others.
